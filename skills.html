About me

  • I am a Bachelor of International Business, specialized in marketing, having graduated from Kymenlaakso University of Applied Sciences (KyUAS). Throughout most of my professional career, I have primarily worked in the marketing and logistics fields. For the past five years, I had the opportunity to contribute my skills and knowledge at Verkkokauppa.com, where I excelled as an Export Manager until the autumn of 2022. However, seeking a new challenge and a change of professional direction, I made the decision to transition into the field of frontend development.

    In pursuit of this goal, I embarked on a journey to learn the fundamentals of web development, starting with HTML and CSS. In January 2023, I enrolled in a comprehensive web programming course facilitated by Omnia. This course spans eight months, encompassing two months of practical training. During the program, I gained valuable insights into various backend technologies such as PHP, Flask, and React. I delved deeper into the realm of styling and tools such as Bootstrap. I acquired the proficiency to create visually appealing and responsive user interfaces.

    With a background in marketing, logistics, and now equipped with frontend and basic backend skills, I am poised to embark on a full-stack development journey. Passionate about creating innovative and user-friendly web solutions, I am excited to contribute my diverse skill set to dynamic and challenging projects.
